Project Site Plan

Web-Based Hotel Management System

```

Site Name

Hotel Operations Management System (HOMS)

This name was selected because it clearly represents a centralized system designed to manage hotel sales, reporting, and front desk operations in a professional and recognizable way.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to demonstrate a responsive, web-based hotel management system that supports front desk operations, sales tracking, room status monitoring, and administrative reporting. The site will serve as both an academic project and a conceptual foundation for a real-world hotel management application.

Scenarios

  • How can front desk staff quickly record guest check-ins and check-outs while updating room availability?
  • How can hotel administrators view daily sales summaries and room occupancy reports from a single dashboard?
  • Can the system be accessed easily on mobile devices during front desk operations?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Blue (#0b3d91): Used for headers, section titles, navigation elements, and accents.
  • Light Gray (#f6f6f6): Used as the main page background for readability.
  • White (#ffffff): Used for content sections and cards.

Typography

Roboto is used as the primary font for the entire document.

  • Headings: Roboto Bold
  • Body text: Roboto Regular

This font was selected for its clarity, modern appearance, and strong readability across devices.
```
